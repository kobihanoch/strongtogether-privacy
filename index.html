Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 28, 2025

Introduction

Strong Together ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Full name, username, and email address
  • Birthdate, gender, and profile image (if uploaded)
  • Workout data: exercises, sets, reps, weights, dates
  • Cardio logs and session details
  • Device ID and push notification token
  • App version, crash reports, and technical diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To save your workouts and progress history
  • To show you personalized statistics and insights
  • To send relevant push notifications (if enabled)
  • To improve app stability, performance, and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Some information is processed through trusted third-party services such as:

  • Hosting and storage (e.g. Render, Supabase)
  • Push notification services (e.g. Expo Notifications)

All third-party services are required to meet high standards of data protection and confidentiality.

4. Data Security

  • All communication is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Authentication tokens are securely stored
  • We implement safeguards to reduce data breach risks

5. Retention and Deletion

You may request to delete your account and all associated data at any time, either within the app (if supported) or by emailing us. Deletion requests are processed within 14 business days after identity verification.

6. Your Rights

  • Access a copy of your stored data
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent for notifications at any time
