Privacy Policy – Strong Together

Last updated: September 28, 2025

1. Overview

Strong Together (“the App”) is a fitness tracking mobile application that respects your privacy. This policy explains what data I collect, how I use it, and your rights.

2. Information I Collect

  • User profile: full name, username, email, birthdate, gender, profile image
  • Authentication: access token (JWT), refresh token, secure login sessions
  • Workout data: workout plans, splits, exercises, sets, reps, weights, notes
  • Cardio tracking: type, duration, workout date
  • Scheduled workouts: reminders, time, day of week, user preferences
  • Messages: inbox messages sent between users
  • Push notifications: push_token for device notifications
  • Technical data: app version, crash logs, device info

3. How I Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and manage secure sessions
  • To track and display your workouts and fitness progress
  • To send reminders or workout-related notifications (if enabled)
  • To analyze your workout trends and progress (locally or in backend)
  • To support core features like messaging, exercise history, statistics
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance your experience

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS
  • JWT tokens are stored securely using SecureStore
  • Passwords are hashed before storage in Supabase
  • Redis is used in the backend for performance and queuing (non-personal)
  • I follow best practices to prevent unauthorized access

5. Third-Party Services

Your data is handled securely using the following services:

  • Supabase – database, authentication, and storage
  • Render – hosting backend server
  • Redis – in-memory cache (for performance only)
  • Expo Notifications – sending push notifications

These services comply with modern security and privacy standards.

6. Account Deletion

You may delete your account directly from within the app at any time. Upon confirmation, your profile and all associated data (including workout history, cardio logs, and scheduled sessions) are deleted immediately. This process is irreversible.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Disable push notifications at any time via app or OS settings
  • Contact me at any time regarding privacy concerns

8. Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact: kobikobi622@gmail.com

Note: Strong Together does not integrate with Apple Health or HealthKit at this time. If I do in the future, this policy will be updated, and your explicit consent will be requested.
